Background & lock-screen playback

ShinPlay keeps a silent audio session and screen wake-lock active so playback keeps going when you switch apps or the screen dims, and lock-screen media controls (play/pause/skip) are wired up. On Android Chrome this reliably keeps audio playing with the screen off. iOS Safari and some battery-saver modes may still pause playback in the background — that's a platform restriction on embedded video, not a ShinPlay setting.

About downloading

ShinPlay doesn't rip or download YouTube videos directly — extracting video files bypasses YouTube's systems and breaks its Terms of Service and creators' copyright, so that isn't something this app does.
